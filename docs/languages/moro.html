The glyphs used in the alphabet above reflect glyph usage in the Moro readers and primers produced in the early to mid 2000s, and in a draft Moro-English dictionary.

The green glyphs are variant glyphs. The red glyph is a variant that is unsupported by existing Unicode fonts.
